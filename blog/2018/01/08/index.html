  1. Home
  2. The Blog

8 January 2018, Neil Parkhi

Introduction

The Tahoe and Suburban have always been important SUVs to Chevrolet, and the new RST or Rally Sport Truck Package is available on both the LT and Premier (formerly LTZ) model of both the cars. The RST has special performance equipment including the 420 brake horsepower from the 6.2 litre V8 from the 2018 GMC Yukon Denali and Cadillac Escalade and an all new 10-speed automatic transmission.
